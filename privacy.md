# Privacy Policy for SideQuest

**Last Updated:** December 22, 2025

## Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how SideQuest ("we," "our," or "us") handles information when you use our mobile application ("the App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

## Information We Collect

### Camera and Photo Access
- The App requests permission to access your device's camera to enable you to take photos and videos.
- The App requests permission to access your photo library to allow you to select and share photos.
- **All photos and videos are processed locally on your device.** We do not upload, store, or transmit your photos or videos to our servers or third parties.

### Audio Recording
- The App requests permission to record audio (for video recording functionality when using the camera).
- Audio is captured only when you actively use the video recording feature.
- **Audio recordings are processed locally on your device and are not transmitted to our servers.**

### Device Information
- The App may collect standard device information (device model, operating system version) necessary for app functionality and compatibility.
- This information is collected through the Expo framework to ensure proper app operation.

## How We Use Your Information

- **Camera/Photo Data:** Used solely to provide camera and photo selection functionality within the App.
- **Audio Data:** Used only for video recording features when you choose to record videos.
- **Device Information:** Used for app functionality, compatibility, and technical support purposes.

## Data Storage and Security

- **Local Storage Only:** Photos, videos, and audio captured or selected in the App are stored only on your device. We do not maintain servers that store your media files.
- **No Cloud Storage:** We do not upload, backup, or sync your photos, videos, or audio to any cloud service or remote server.
- **Security:** While we implement reasonable security measures within the App, the security of your device and its storage is primarily your responsibility.

## Third-Party Services

### Expo SDK
- The App is built using Expo SDK, which may collect anonymous usage data and crash reports to improve the SDK.
- Expo's data collection practices are governed by Expo's Privacy Policy: https://expo.dev/privacy
- We recommend reviewing Expo's Privacy Policy for information about their data practices.

### Expo Application Services (EAS)
- If you receive app updates through EAS, update checks may involve communication with Expo's servers.
- This communication is limited to technical data necessary for update delivery.

## Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We do not share your photos, videos, or audio recordings with any third parties, except:
- As required by law or legal process
- To protect our rights or the safety of our users

## Your Rights and Choices

### Permissions
- You can revoke camera, photo library, or audio recording permissions at any time through your device's settings.
- Revoking permissions may limit certain App functionality.

### Data Deletion
- Since we do not store your photos, videos, or audio on our servers, no data deletion request is necessary.
- To remove data from your device, you can:
  - Delete photos/videos from your device's gallery
  - Uninstall the App
  - Revoke App permissions
